# Executive KPI Dashboard Plan

## Objective
Build a visually polished executive dashboard that highlights top-line performance, profitability, trend movement, and business drivers using public sales data.

## Target Audience
Senior leadership / finance leadership

## Core Questions
1. How is revenue trending over time?
2. Which categories and products drive the most sales?
3. What is the profit and margin profile?
4. Which regions or segments perform best?
5. Where are the biggest performance opportunities or risks?

## Core KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Average Order Value
- Order Count
- Revenue Growth %
- Top Category
- Top Region

## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Summary
2. Sales & Profit Trends
3. Category / Product Performance
4. Regional Performance
5. Key Insights & Recommendations

## Design Standard
- Clean
- Executive-ready
- Minimal clutter
- Strong KPI cards
- Consistent color palette
- Easy to understand in under 60 seconds
