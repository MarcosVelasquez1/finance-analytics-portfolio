# 13-Week Cash Flow Model

This project will showcase a professional 13-week cash flow model designed to track weekly inflows, outflows, and projected liquidity.

## Goal
Build a highly practical cash flow tool that helps monitor short-term cash position, working capital timing, and funding needs.

## Focus Areas
- Weekly cash receipts
- Weekly cash disbursements
- Net cash movement
- Beginning and ending cash balance
- Liquidity risk visibility

## Tools
- Excel
- Power BI

In progress
